Homelab philosophy

A homelab is a place to experiment, learn, break, fix, traverse the throes of failure and transcend into the heaven of having-fixed-it. It's a wonderful adventure. With my homelab, I am always trying to expand, learn something new, push silicon and push myself as a human, have fun, be creative, make poetry of it. More practically, I play around with:

  • Network segmentation + controlled remote access
  • Virtualization + services + monitoring (iterative)
  • Coding, automation, using AI.
  • Learning through building: logs, detection thinking, hardening
  • Expressing myself. Use Your Imagination.

Writing & communication

I have been writing Hebrew poetry for almost all my life. I have published 4 books of poetry, and am awaiting the publicaition of my first novel. Besides that, I've worked for around 20 years in legal translation. I love texts. I love writing, documenting, communication. Check out my documentation projects (under my HomeLabKit repo, or in my HomeSweetLab Youtube channel) to see my approach to documentation and communication. I love a good story or at least that's what I tell myself.
